About $KSTATE

$KSTATE is the official meme token for Kansas State University, developed by CS students on Solana. It unites K-Staters under one playful, community-driven project.

Tokenomics

  • Max Supply: 1,000,000,000 $KSTATE
  • Blockchain: Solana
  • Distribution: Airdrops, Community Rewards, Liquidity Pool
  • Use Cases: Merchandise, Giveaways, Meme Contests

Roadmap

  1. Q1 2025: Launch $KSTATE & Airdrop to K-State community
  2. Q2 2025: Meme contest & marketing campaign
  3. Q3 2025: Partnerships with campus clubs, merch store
  4. Q4 2025: DEX listing & further expansions
